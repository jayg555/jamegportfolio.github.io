About Me

I am a full-stack developer who has always been interested in tinkering with technology. When I enlisted in the military I realized that I have a keen eye for attention to detail. All through my career I discovered my passion for working in team based environments. It has taught me that working towards a common goal through open communication and collaboration is invaluable. With those skills ingrained in me throughout the years I discovered that those same soft skills are great for programming. Creating abstract lines of code and seeing that displayed on a screen made me realize how cool programming is. As a veteran I discovered that there was a big employment gap with veterans working in the technology field and realized that this is the field I want to be in for the rest of my life.
